How It Works
Three steps from zero to selling
1

Download Pocket Shop Studio

Get the desktop app for macOS or Windows. Or run it from the terminal with npm.

2

Enter Your Product Details

Name, description, price, image. Enter your Minima address and public key. Click Build.

3

Deploy & Sell

Install your inbox on your node. Publish the shop to PocketWeb. It's instantly accessible by address and searchable by keyword — no need to share a link.
